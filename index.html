About

Welcome to the Hass Lab website! We really need a better name for the lab, and we're working on it!

We are (actually 'I am', currently) a new lab at the Emory University College of Medicine and this is our website!

The Hass lab studies metabolism in the retina and in the retinal pigment epithelium. We use a combination of isotopic tracers and mass spectrometry.
